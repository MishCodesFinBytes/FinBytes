An ode to elegance, structure, and timelessness.

Massimo is a theme that reverences the legendary designer Massimo Vignelli. It reproduces one of his famous projects, a series of posters for a famous theater in Milan.

Due to his fine experimentation, which echoes the communication of the 1960s, his design choices are still suitable for promoting programs, calendars, and similar events.
